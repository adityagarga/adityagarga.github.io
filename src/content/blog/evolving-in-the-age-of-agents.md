# Fake plagiarism (cheating but thinking everyone's getting away with it)
LLM's give us the illusion to be able to cheat at things that always seemed difficult. Like doing extremley well at your job with less effort, learning and mastering difficult skills or trying to build that business you always wanted to run. 

Difficult things are just plain difficult, and I don't think that will change in the age of agents. I think most people realize pretty early on, that their aren't any cheat codes like in GTA (some of them are still etched in my muscle memory 18 years later ...).
